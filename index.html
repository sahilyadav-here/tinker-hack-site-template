< About TinkerHACK'18 >

  • Date:2nd November 2018
  • Theme:Internet of Things and Associated Spheres
  • Venue:3rd floor, Seminar Building , IIITD

Tinker Hackathon is an initiative by IIITD ECE department, IEEE Student Chapter and VLSID2019 To promote hardware hacking and help in building hardware solutions. We aim to Bring people from different communities together under one roof, providing mentorship and support to make your ideas come to life.

This is your time to dust off old ideas and try something entirely new. Imagine the craziest projects possible. Hack your way from concept to designing a cool hardware-based product. Inexperienced hackers may often learn some new skills by participating in a hackathon.
